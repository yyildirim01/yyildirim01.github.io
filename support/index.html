Frequently asked questions

Is my data safe?

Kees does not sell your personal data. Speech and photo inputs are only used to power pronunciation feedback and text scanning features.

Why does Kees ask for microphone access?

The microphone is used for speaking exercises so Kees can give you pronunciation feedback. You can decline this permission, but speaking exercises won't work without it.

Why does Kees ask for camera / photo access?

The camera and photo library are used for the "Scan & Learn" feature, which recognizes Dutch text in images so you can translate and save new words.

How do I report a bug or request a feature?

Send us an email using the button above with as much detail as possible (what happened, what you expected, and your device model).

Can I use Kees offline?

Most learning content works offline. Speech recognition and some pronunciation features may require an internet connection.
